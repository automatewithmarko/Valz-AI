**Slide 4 - Name the Invisible Problem**

The part that rarely gets spoken about is that the guilt has almost nothing to do with the baby.

It's about having been taught, very quietly, that a good mother disappears into her role. That needing something for yourself is a sign you're not doing it properly.

---

**Slide 5 - Cultural Expectation**

Somehow we're meant to recover physically while also being endlessly available.

To prioritise our health, but only if it doesn't inconvenience anyone. To take care of ourselves, as long as it's fast, silent, and doesn't look like enjoyment.
