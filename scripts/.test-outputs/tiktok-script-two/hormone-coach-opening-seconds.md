## How to ship this

- Layer your voiceover under a trending sound from your FYP right now, ideally something mid-tempo that's repeating across health and wellness creators. Native audio signals help the algorithm slot you into the right category faster.
- Make this the first in a recurring format: "Your cycle explains that" (same hook structure, same 25-second length, different symptom each week). Pattern repetition trains both your audience and the algorithm.
- Post, then go live within roughly an hour talking about which phase most women are in right now and what that means for their energy this week. The live gives the fresh post a second push.
