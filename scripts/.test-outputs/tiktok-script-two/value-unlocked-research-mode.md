## How to ship this

- **Trending audio.** Record your voiceover clean, then layer it under a trending sound from your FYP. Pick something appearing repeatedly in the business or self-development corner of your feed. Native sound gets the algorithm to categorise you faster.
- **Make it repeatable.** This works as the first in a "you already know enough" series. Same hook energy, same talking-head format, different angle each time. Pattern recognition trains both the algorithm and your audience.
- **Post, then go live.** Within roughly an hour of posting, go live and talk about what's actually stopping people from launching their first offer. TikTok pushes the fresh video harder when it can funnel viewers into a live.
